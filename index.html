Game Info

How to Play

Place the printed markers on a flat surface.
Enemies will spawn and move towards the base. Towers will automatically attack enemies within range. Defend your base by moving tower markers on your table and purchasing upgrades using energy earned from defeating enemies.
Can you survive all 10 waves?

Save Data

Your energy and upgrades are automatically saved. If any issues occur, simply reload the page and your progress will be restored.
